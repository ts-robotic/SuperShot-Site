Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for speed and precision. No bloat, no cloud accounts, no subscriptions.

Built-in Screen Recording

Drag a box over any region and record it straight to MP4 — with your microphone. A 3-2-1 countdown, live timer, and one-key stop. No screen-share prompts, no second app.

Instant Region Capture

One hotkey triggers a pixel-perfect region selector with crosshair cursor and live dimension labels. Select exactly what you need.

Annotation Toolkit

Pencil, Line, Arrow, Rectangle, and Text tools with 12 colour presets and adjustable line widths. Mark up screenshots in seconds.

Copy, Save & Share

Copy to clipboard with one click, save to disk as PNG or JPEG, print directly, or pin screenshots on top of other windows.

Multi-Display Support

Automatically detects which display your cursor is on and captures at native resolution. Full DPI scaling support for HiDPI screens.

Customisable Hotkeys

Remap every shortcut to your preference. Region, full screen, scrolling and timed capture each have their own global hotkey.

Privacy First

No cloud, no accounts, no telemetry. Your screenshots stay on your machine. EV code-signed by Mortgage Magick Ltd for verified trust.

Clipboard History

Keeps your last 20 captures in memory for quick access from the system tray. Never lose a screenshot again.

Delayed Capture

Set a 3, 5, or 10 second timer before capture. Perfect for capturing menus, tooltips, and hover states that disappear on click.

Pin to Screen

Pin any capture as a floating always-on-top window. Resize, adjust opacity, and keep reference images visible while you work.

How It Works

Three steps. Under a second.

1

Press the Hotkey

Hit Ctrl+Shift+S, or open the recorder from the app. The screen freezes and a crosshair appears instantly.

2

Select & Annotate

Click and drag to select your region. Annotation tools appear automatically — draw arrows, add text, highlight areas.

3

Copy or Save

One click to copy to clipboard, save to disk, print, or pin on screen. Your screenshot is ready to share.
