Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for speed and precision — no bloat, no cloud accounts, no subscriptions.

Built-in Screen Recording

Drag a box over any region and record it straight to MP4 — with your microphone. A 3-2-1 countdown, live timer, one-key stop. No screen-share prompts, no second app.

Instant Region Capture

One hotkey triggers a pixel-perfect region selector with crosshair cursor and live dimension labels. Select exactly what you need.

Live, Editable Annotation

Pencil, line, arrow, rectangle, ellipse and text — with shapes that preview as you drag and text that stays editable and draggable until you save.

Scrolling Capture

Capture long pages frame by frame — SuperShot detects the overlap and stitches them into one seamless, tall screenshot.

Multi-Monitor & HiDPI

Records the region wherever it sits, on any display, at native resolution. Full DPI scaling support for sharp HiDPI screens.

Private & Verified

No cloud, no accounts, no telemetry — your captures stay on your machine. EV code-signed by Mortgage Magick Ltd for verified trust.

How it works

Three steps. Under a second.

1

Press the hotkey

Hit Ctrl+Shift+S, or open the recorder from the app. The screen freezes and a crosshair appears instantly.

2

Select & mark up

Drag to select a region. Annotation tools appear automatically — or start recording the same selection to MP4.

3

Copy, save or record

One click to copy, save or pin a screenshot — or press Esc to finish a recording. Done.

Keyboard shortcuts
