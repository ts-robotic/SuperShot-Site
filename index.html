Capture modes

Four ways to capture.
One key to record.

Region, full screen, scrolling and timed capture, live annotation, and region recording to MP4. All of it in one lightweight, native app.

Record · MP4 + mic

Region recording to MP4

Draw a box over any part of the screen and record it straight to MP4 with microphone audio. A short countdown leads in, a live timer keeps you honest, and one key stops and saves. No screen-share prompts, no second app.

Region

Region capture

One hotkey, a crosshair and live dimension labels. Drag across exactly the pixels you need and release.

Full screen

Full-screen capture

The whole display in one keystroke, at native resolution on every monitor, with full HiDPI scaling support.

Scrolling

Scrolling capture

Capture long pages frame by frame. SuperShot detects the overlap and stitches one tall, continuous image.

Timed

Timed capture

Set a short delay, open the menu or tooltip you could never screenshot, and SuperShot fires the capture for you.

Annotate

Annotation that stays editable

Pencil, line, arrow, rectangle, ellipse and text. Shapes preview as you drag, and text stays editable and draggable until you save.

How it works

From hotkey to saved shot in seconds.

STEP 01

Press the hotkey

Hit Ctrl Shift S anywhere in Windows. The screen freezes and a crosshair appears instantly.

STEP 02

Drag the region

Select exactly what you need, guided by live dimension labels. Annotation tools appear the moment you release.

STEP 03

Save, copy or record

Copy, save or pin the shot, or record the same selection to MP4 and press Esc when you are done.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is SuperShot free?

Yes. SuperShot is free to download from the Microsoft Store, with no subscription, no watermarks and no account.

Which versions of Windows does SuperShot support?

SuperShot runs on Windows 10 and Windows 11 (64-bit). It is a native build designed to stay fast on modest hardware.

Does SuperShot upload my screenshots or recordings?

No. Captures and recordings are saved on your device and stay there. SuperShot has no telemetry, no analytics and no advertising.

Can SuperShot record my screen with audio?

Yes. Select any region of the screen and record it straight to MP4 with microphone audio. A countdown leads in, a live timer runs while you record, and one key stops it.

Is there a Mac or mobile version of SuperShot?

No. SuperShot is built for Windows only.
